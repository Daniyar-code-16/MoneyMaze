01 — Project Description

What is MoneyMaze and why does it matter?

💸

The Problem

74% of teens lack confidence in managing money. Schools teach algebra but not how to file taxes, build credit, or avoid debt. Financial illiteracy costs individuals over $1,500 annually in fees and poor decisions.

💡

Our Solution

MoneyMaze is an interactive educational game that simulates real financial decisions. Players navigate monthly budgets, invest in stocks, handle emergencies, and build wealth — all in a risk-free environment.

🎯

Why It's Important

Financial habits form early. By making financial education engaging and accessible, we're helping the next generation avoid predatory loans, build credit wisely, and achieve financial independence.

02 — Functional Requirements

Core features of MoneyMaze

🎮

Gameplay Systems

  • Monthly budget simulation with random life events
  • Stock market mini-game with real historical data
  • Credit score system that affects loan approval
  • Emergency expense generator (car repair, medical bill)
  • Multiplayer challenges: who can save the most?
  • Achievement system for financial milestones
📚

Educational Features

  • Interactive glossary of 50+ financial terms
  • Real-life scenarios based on actual case studies
  • Progress tracking for parents and teachers
  • Three difficulty levels: Beginner → Expert
  • Localized content for Kazakhstan, US, and Europe
  • Video tutorials from financial advisors
👤

User Systems

  • Secure registration and login
  • Cloud save across devices
  • Parent dashboard with progress reports
  • Multi-language support (EN/RU/KZ)
  • Accessibility features for all users
  • Social features: compete with friends
